Privacy Policy

At JobKonnect, we prioritize the privacy and security of our users. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website. By accessing and using JobKonnect, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

When you create an account on JobKonnect or use our services, we may collect certain information from you, including:

  • Your name, email address, and contact details
  • Your resume, cover letter, and other application materials
  • Your employment history, skills, and qualifications
  • Information related to job preferences and search criteria
  • Any other information you choose to provide us
We may also collect non-personal information such as IP addresses, browser type, device information, and website usage data to improve our services and enhance user experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Facilitate the job search and recruitment process
  • Match job seekers with relevant job opportunities
  • Enable employers to review candidate profiles and contact potential hires
  • Improve our services and personalize user experience
  • Communicate with users regarding their accounts, job applications, and relevant updates
  • Aggregate data for statistical and analytical purposes
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and protect the rights and safety of our users

Sharing of Information

We may share your personal information with:

  • Employers and recruiters when you apply for a job
  • Service providers and partners who assist us in delivering our services
  • Third parties as required by law or to protect our legal rights
We do not sell, rent, or lease your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes without your consent.

Data Security

We take appropriate measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
